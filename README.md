### 👨‍🔬 About me

- Ph.D. in Agricultural Statistics (ICAR‑IASRI, New Delhi) specialising in **time series**, machine learning, and deep learning for agri‑food systems.  
- CEO & Research Scientist at AVYAGRAHA RESEARCH & ANALYTICS LLP, delivering data‑driven solutions in agriculture, health, environment, and finance.  
- I work at the interface of modern AI (Transformers, GANs, deep time‑series models) and real‑world problems: climate resilience, agri‑price volatility, remote sensing, and health analytics.  
- Research interests: spatial statistics, Bayesian methods, generative AI, LLMs, quantum computing, and blockchain for data‑driven decision making.

### 🧠 Skills & tools

- Statistics & ML: Time series (ARIMA, SARIMA, GARCH, HAR, HMMs), regression, multivariate analysis (PCA, clustering, factor analysis, discriminant analysis), design of experiments.
